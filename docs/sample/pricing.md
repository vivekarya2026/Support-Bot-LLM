# AgentPay Pricing

> **PLACEHOLDER CONTENT.** Replace with real pricing before launch.

AgentPay charges per transaction, with volume tiers and a free developer tier.

## Tiers

### Developer (Free)
- Up to 100 transactions / month
- Sandbox environments only
- Community support
- No SLA

### Startup — $49 / month
- Up to 5,000 transactions / month
- Production environment
- Email support, 48h response
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- $0.012 per transaction over the included amount

### Growth — $299 / month
- Up to 50,000 transactions / month
- Multiple production environments
- Priority email + Slack support, 8h response
- 99.95% uptime SLA
- $0.008 per transaction over the included amount

### Enterprise — Custom
- Unlimited transactions
- Dedicated infrastructure, custom regions
- 24/7 support with named contact
- 99.99% uptime SLA, custom contracts
- Custom per-transaction pricing
- Contact sales@agentpay.example for a quote

## What counts as a transaction?

A transaction is any authorization request submitted to AgentPay, whether
it is approved, denied, or escalated. Failed authorizations due to AgentPay
service errors (5xx responses) are not counted.

## Currency support

AgentPay supports USD, EUR, GBP, and CAD at GA. Additional currencies
(JPY, AUD, INR) are in private beta.
